Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I track my parcel?
    Go to the Track Parcel page and enter your 10-digit ID.
  • What happens if I miss the delivery?
    We will automatically attempt redelivery on the next business day.
  • How do I change my billing address?
    You can update your address in the Profile Settings tab.
